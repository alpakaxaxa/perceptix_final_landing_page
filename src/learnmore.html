Why to measure the implicit

Whether due to socially desirable response behavior, or due to the fact that people are not aware of their opinion, explicit statements have limited explanatory power. Be it for stereotypes or in the area of marketing and brand positioning, reaction-based tests can identify subconscious associations and thus generate significantly more insight.

Implicit bias training

Increase the learning experience & measure the effetiveness of your interventions

Branding

Track your brand health and brand assets or see if your brand is top of mind in your industry

HR Reporting

Put the implicit on the HR reporting agenda and analyse unconscious bias throughout your organization

Marketing

Test your adds, packaging or propositions and see how they are perceived on the subconsious level
